IMPORTANT UNIFORM INFORMATION!

EFFECTIVE 2012 SEASON

Goalkeepers Uniform
- Goalkeepers will be required to have a number on the back of their jerseys and the front of their jerseys or shorts that is different than any other number used by other players on the team. (NFHS Rule 4-1-1-i-1)
Shinguards
- Beginning with the 2012 Fall season, the NOCSAE seal and height range shall be permanently marked on the front of the shinguard. Equipment shall not be modified from its original manufactured state and shall be worn in the manner the manufacturer intended it to be worn. It is also recommended that male players wear a supporter and protective cup. (NFHS Rule 4-1-1)
